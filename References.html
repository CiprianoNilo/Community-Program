CONCLUSION

Typhoons are natural phenomena that we people cannot avoid. Preparedness is the key to minimize the destruction they will provide. Right planning, right response will help people to survive if this happens. Being prepared all the time since there are warnings, or participating in the earthquake drill will help you focus when the time of calamity happens.
