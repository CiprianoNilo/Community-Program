INTRODUCTION

The Philippines is located in an area affected by natural disasters called the Ring of Fire or Typhoon Belt. This is because the Philippines is near the Pacific Ocean region where there are a great number of volcanic eruptions and earthquakes. These are the dangerous natural events that we are experiencing in the Philippines: Typhoons, Volcanic eruptions, Earthquakes and Secondary effects.

Typhoons

Every year, 19 typhoons enter the Philippine region and about 7 or 8 make landfall. In the past, some have been extraordinarily powerful and caused casualties and the collapse of infrastructures in the affected area
